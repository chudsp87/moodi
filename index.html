Welcome to Moodi!

At Moodi we're dedicated to helping inviduals be better understand others' emotions and be able to express on the outside what they feel on the inside. Moodi glasses enable the wearer to visually see other Moodi-users' emotion/mood/availability thru a colored "aura" or "glow" that envelops the individual and corresponds to that user's current mood.

In order to best serve you, there is some preliminary information we need to gather from you to get everything rolling. There's also several features we'd like to quickly share with you so can easily navigate the app to make any changes that you may wish to in the future.

So if you're ready, let's get started!
